Features

Everything your operation needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Built by someone who worked in the field. Every feature solves a real problem faced on the ground.

⊡
QR Code Clock-In
Each site gets a unique QR code. Employees scan it to clock in — no QR scan, no clock-in. Physical presence confirmed every time.
📍
GPS Radius Verification
Employees must be within your defined GPS radius to clock in. If they're not at the site, the app blocks them. No exceptions.
🔐
PIN Authentication
Every clock-in requires a personal 4-digit PIN. No buddy punching. Each entry is tied to a specific employee identity.
🟢
Live Manager Dashboard
See who's on duty, at which site, and for how long — in real time. Full view of your entire operation from one screen.
🔔
SMS Late Alerts
Get a text when an employee hasn't clocked in by their scheduled start. Set your own grace period. Never miss a no-show again.
📋
Payroll-Ready Export
Export any pay period with regular vs overtime hours per employee. Drop it straight into ADP, Paychex, Gusto, or any payroll provider.
📅
Shift Scheduling
Schedule employees to specific sites in advance. Late alerts automatically check actual clock-ins against your schedule.
⏱
Session Tokens
QR scans generate a 5-minute session token. Employees must complete clock-in within that window — no screenshot abuse possible.
📱
No App Download
Works on any phone browser instantly. Employees open the link, scan, and clock in. Zero friction, zero IT setup required.
How It Works

Up and running in minutes.

Four simple steps from setup to your first verified clock-in.

1
Add Your Sites
Enter your site name, GPS coordinates, and radius in the manager dashboard. About 60 seconds per site.
2
Print QR Codes
Generate and print a unique QR code for each site directly from the dashboard. Post it at the location.
3
Employees Scan In
Employees scan the QR, GPS confirms location, PIN confirms identity. Clocked in — all in under 10 seconds.
4
You See It Live
Monitor every employee in real time. Export payroll when you need it. Get alerted if someone doesn't show up.
