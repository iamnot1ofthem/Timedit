Features

Everything your operation needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Built by someone who worked security. Every feature solves a real problem we faced on the ground.

⊡
QR Code Clock-In
Each site gets a unique QR code. Guards scan it to clock in — no QR scan, no clock-in. Physical presence confirmed every time.
📍
GPS Radius Verification
Guards must be within your defined GPS radius to clock in. If they're not at the site, the app blocks them. No exceptions.
🔐
PIN Authentication
Every clock-in requires a personal 4-digit PIN. No buddy punching. Each entry is tied to a specific employee identity.
🟢
Live Supervisor Dashboard
See who's on duty, at which site, and for how long — in real time. Full view of your entire operation from one screen.
📋
Shift Records & History
Every shift is logged with clock-in/out times, duration, and location. Filter by guard, date, or site. Full audit trail always available.
⬇
One-Click Payroll Export
Export any date range as a CSV with guard names, hours, and sites. Drop it straight into your payroll system. No manual entry.
⏱
Session Tokens
QR scans generate a 5-minute session token. Guards must complete clock-in within that window — preventing QR screenshot abuse.
🖨
Printable QR Codes
Generate and print a QR code for any site in seconds from the supervisor dashboard. Post it and guards are ready to go.
📱
Mobile-First Design
Works perfectly on any phone — no app store download required. Just open the link, scan, and clock in. Zero friction.
How It Works
