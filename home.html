Features

Everything your operation needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Built by someone who worked security. Every feature solves a real problem faced on the ground.

⊡
QR Code Clock-In
Each site gets a unique QR code. Guards scan it to clock in — no QR scan, no clock-in. Physical presence confirmed every time.
📍
GPS Radius Verification
Guards must be within your defined GPS radius to clock in. If they're not at the site, the app blocks them. No exceptions.
🔐
PIN Authentication
Every clock-in requires a personal 4-digit PIN. No buddy punching. Each entry is tied to a specific employee identity.
🟢
Live Supervisor Dashboard
See who's on duty, at which site, and for how long — in real time. Full view of your entire operation from one screen.
🔔
SMS Late Alerts
Get a text when a guard hasn't clocked in by their scheduled start. Set your own grace period. Never miss a no-show again.
📋
Payroll-Ready Export
Export any pay period with regular vs overtime hours per guard. Drop it straight into ADP, Paychex, Gusto, or any payroll provider.
📅
Shift Scheduling
Schedule guards to specific sites in advance. Late alerts automatically check actual clock-ins against your schedule.
⏱
Session Tokens
QR scans generate a 5-minute session token. Guards must complete clock-in within that window — no screenshot abuse possible.
📱
No App Download
Works on any phone browser instantly. Guards open the link, scan, and clock in. Zero friction, zero IT setup required.
How It Works
